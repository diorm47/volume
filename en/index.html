Any questions?

An artificial intelligence (AI) model evaluates numerous factors to determine when to initiate or conclude a trade. The parameters used by the AI are established by machine learning experts in the form of datasets. Additionally, the model undergoes training using data from day traders. For more detailed information about the operational algorithm, please refer to our Whitepaper.

The service is accessible with a deposit balance of $100 or higher. To generate income and simultaneously cover the subscription cost, we suggest utilizing a minimum of $250 - $500. Additionally, we advise against using more than $3,000 in the initial month.

We highly discourage using borrowed funds beyond your means. While we have confidence in our capabilities, engaging in cryptocurrency trading not only offers potential for high returns but also entails a risk of loss. Only invest funds that you are prepared to lose. The US Securities and Exchange Commission has established guidelines for prudent and responsible investing, which we recommend reading at https://www.sec.gov/investor/pubs/sec-guide-to-savings-and-investing.pdf

We do not impose transaction fees or take a percentage of earnings. To utilize the service, a subscription must be purchased. The subscription cost is contingent on the investment amount: the larger the deposit, the higher the subscription cost. In the future, users with deposits exceeding $5,000 may be eligible for a profit-based subscription.

We aim to launch without user limits by mid-2024.
